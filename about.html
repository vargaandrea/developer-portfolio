About Me

I got my first computer when I was 12 years old, and since then I have been hooked up to computer science and programming. In high school I was a frequent visitor of the physics lab, spending there long afternoons and many weekends, where we implemented many computer controlled experiments, very cool stuff, using low level programming languages like Assembly (needed to be fast), but also Pascal and C. It was still DOS time then :). I gathered a very good basic knowledge and a way of thinking that helped me a lot later. At the university I studied a lot of programming languages like: C/C++, Java, Lisp, Prolog. I enjoyed the diversity of the topics, but what I enjoyed the most were the practical assignments that we did in pairs, where we had the possibility to combine our ideas and knowledge.
